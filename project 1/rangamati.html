Most Beautiful Rangamati Tour

Rangamati is a small town in the Chittagong Division and a district in south-eastern Bangladesh. Rangamati is covered with green hills, lakes, and rivers. For this reason, tourists from Bangladesh and abroad like the place to pass their free time. It is situated at the side of Kaptai Lake, so Rangamati is considered the Lake City Of Bangladesh. It is essential to realize that it is the largest district in Bangladesh, just 77 km from Chittagong town. For the tradition of the tribal people, scenic beauty, type of locations, and natural resources, including diverse flora and fauna, have made the place more attractive.
